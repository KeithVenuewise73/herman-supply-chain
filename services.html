Service Offerings

Every Tool You Need to Optimize Your Supply Chain

We offer a comprehensive suite of logistics and supply chain consulting services — from forensic cost analysis to full-scale operational transformation.

01

Routing Efficiency Analysis

Our most requested service. We conduct a forensic review of your entire route network — stop density, geographic clustering, time-window constraints, and vehicle utilization. We model alternatives, quantify cost-per-stop by lane, and redesign route structures to minimize empty miles, reduce delivery windows, and increase stops-per-route ratios. Clients typically achieve 12–22% transportation cost reduction through route restructuring alone.

Route Engineering Stop Density Mile Reduction Vehicle Utilization Geographic Clustering
🗺
02

Operational Cost Audits

A comprehensive line-by-line audit of all transportation and logistics costs. We examine fuel efficiency by vehicle and route, carrier contract terms, accessorial charges, detention fees, maintenance cost patterns, and overhead allocation. We benchmark your cost structure against comparable operations nationally and identify where you're overpaying, undercharging, or leaving money on the table.

Fuel Analysis Carrier Contracts Overhead Audit Cost Benchmarking Accessorial Fees
🔍
03

Revenue Recovery Audits

Most logistics operations leak revenue every week without knowing it. Our revenue recovery audit identifies unbilled stops, inaccurate accessorial charges, missed fuel surcharge calculations, billing errors, and customer pricing discrepancies. On average, clients recover 2–6% of annual gross revenue through systematic billing and contract compliance review.

Billing Accuracy Unbilled Stops Fuel Surcharges Contract Compliance Revenue Leakage
💰
04

Labor Optimization

Labor is often the largest and most controllable cost in transportation operations. We analyze driver scheduling, overtime patterns, start-time alignment with route demand, and contractor vs. employee cost structures. We model alternative scheduling frameworks, identify productivity gaps at the driver level, and design incentive structures that align driver behavior with operational efficiency goals.

Schedule Optimization Overtime Reduction Driver Productivity Employee vs. Contractor Incentive Design
👷
05

Driver & Contractor Performance Analysis

Individual driver and contractor performance varies widely in most operations — and the bottom quartile often drives a disproportionate share of costs. We build performance profiles for each driver and contractor, analyzing on-time rates, stop completion, fuel efficiency, vehicle incidents, and customer satisfaction by route. We design structured performance management systems that elevate the entire fleet.

Driver Scorecards Performance Profiles Contractor Analysis CSAT by Route Performance Management
📋
06

KPI Dashboard Development

Real-time operational visibility is the foundation of high-performance supply chains. We design and implement custom KPI command center dashboards that surface the metrics that matter — cost per route, cost per stop, on-time rate, completion rate, revenue per driver, and more. Dashboards are built in your existing tools (Excel, Power BI, Tableau, Google Sheets) with daily automated data feeds where possible.

Power BI Tableau Excel Dashboards Daily KPI Reporting Executive Scorecards
📊
07

Supply Chain Command Center Consulting

Our flagship full-engagement offering. We embed senior consultants within your operation for a structured 90-to-180-day engagement covering all aspects of supply chain optimization: routing, cost, labor, revenue, technology, vendor management, and leadership development. We function as an extension of your executive team — not as external advisors — and stay until measurable results are achieved and sustained.

Full Engagement Executive Embed 90–180 Days End-to-End Optimization Change Management
