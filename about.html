Our Story

30 Years of Logistics Leadership.
Built to Deliver Results.

Herman Supply Chain Solutions was founded on a simple premise: most logistics operations have significant, untapped savings — they just need someone who knows where to look.

Who We Are

Herman Supply Chain Solutions is an independent logistics consulting firm specializing in transportation cost reduction, operational efficiency improvement, and supply chain performance optimization. We serve companies ranging from regional distributors to national 3PL providers across food service, healthcare, retail, industrial, and e-commerce sectors.

Founded in 1994, our firm has completed over 200 operational engagements and recovered more than $180 million in transportation and labor savings for our clients. Our methodology combines forensic cost analysis with practical operational expertise — we don't just identify the problem, we stay until it's solved.

What makes Herman different is our willingness to go deep. We embed with your operations, learn your lanes, understand your drivers, and analyze your costs at the route and stop level — not from 30,000 feet. This ground-level perspective is how we find what others miss.

Our Leadership

Our firm is led by logistics executives with hands-on experience running multi-state transportation networks, directing 3PL operations, and managing fleets of 500+ vehicles. Every engagement is led by a senior consultant with direct carrier, shipper, and operations management experience — not junior analysts learning on your dime.

We bring McKinsey-caliber analytical rigor with the street-level credibility that only comes from decades of actual logistics leadership. Our clients respect us because we've done the job ourselves.
